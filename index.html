Heart

The heart is a muscular organ in the chest that pumps blood throughout the body. It has four chambers: two atria and two ventricles. Blood flows through the heart in a specific sequence: right atrium → right ventricle → lungs (for oxygen), then left atrium → left ventricle → the rest of the body. The heart’s electrical system ensures coordinated contractions, and its valves prevent blood from flowing backward. A healthy heart is crucial for proper circulation and overall health.

Lungs

The lungs are a pair of organs that enable breathing by exchanging oxygen and carbon dioxide.
